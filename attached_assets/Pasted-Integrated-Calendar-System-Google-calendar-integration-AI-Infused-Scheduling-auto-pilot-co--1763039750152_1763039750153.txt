Integrated Calendar System
•	Google calendar integration
•	AI Infused Scheduling, auto pilot & co pilot. AI can suggest or automatically book times during free time in prop mgmt schedule
•	Add ‘waiting showings to be approved' list that AI has booked but waiting your approval. This will be added to the calendar as a ‘pending’ booking where you can accept it or deny.
•	Prop mgmt preferred times for bookings
•	Showing management (move showing times, edit showing title, invite other leads, remove invitees, cancel showing, showing reminders preferability, edit showing description)
•	Showing details (property name, lead, criteria, lead score)
•	Notifications (send gmail and Lead2Lease in-app notifications when showings are booked)
•	Share showing by link to others
•	Create accessible public page where non-users of Lead2Lease can view the showing, accept/decline the showing and view more details

🧠 AI-Powered Scheduling & Optimization
1.	Smart Route Optimization
o	If multiple showings are booked in one day, AI arranges them geographically to minimize travel time.
o	Option to group showings by proximity or unit similarity (e.g., “show all 2-bed units in one block”).
2.	Conflict Avoidance & Auto-Resolution
o	Detect overlapping events (calendar or property) and auto-suggest new available slots.
o	Notify property manager instantly if a time conflict occurs.
3.	Predictive Scheduling
o	AI predicts best times to schedule based on past no-show data and lead availability.
o	AI learns peak conversion hours for each property or neighborhood.
4.	Dynamic Calendar Priority Rules
o	Define “priority blocks” (e.g., weekends = high priority, weekdays after 5 = low priority).
o	Automatically reorder bookings based on urgency or lead score.
📅 Enhanced Booking Management
1.	Bulk Management
o	Edit, cancel, or reschedule multiple showings at once (for weather, maintenance, etc.).
2.	Calendar Color-Coding
o	Visual distinction by property, lead status, or booking type (AI-booked, manual, pending, canceled).
3.	Lead View & Manager View
o	Manager view: all details (lead score, qualification, source, notes).
o	Lead view: simplified schedule and property details only.
4.	Recurring Showings
o	Ability to set recurring open house events or weekly available slots (e.g., “Every Saturday 10–2”).
